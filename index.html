Who We Are

About the College

Lokanayak Omeo Kumar Das College (LOKD College), established in 1970, is one of the most prominent institutions of higher education in the state of Assam. Named after Lokanayak Omeo Kumar Das — a revered political leader and freedom fighter — the college has stood as a beacon of knowledge and character for over five decades.

Affiliated with Gauhati University, the college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programmes across the faculties of Science, Arts, and Commerce. The campus spans a serene environment on the outskirts of Imphal, equipped with modern laboratories, libraries, and sports facilities.

The college has produced thousands of distinguished alumni — civil servants, scientists, doctors, teachers, entrepreneurs, and leaders — who carry its values of integrity, perseverance, and public service into every walk of life.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide quality, affordable, and inclusive higher education that empowers individuals with knowledge, skills, and values — enabling them to contribute meaningfully to society and the nation.

🌟 Our Vision

To be a leading institution of academic excellence in Northeast India — recognized for rigorous scholarship, vibrant campus life, and commitment to social responsibility.

📜 Our History

Founded in 1970 as a tribute to Lokanayak Omeo Kumar Das, the college began with a handful of classrooms and a vision. Today it stands as a fully-fledged institution with modern infrastructure, affiliated to Manipur University and accredited by NAAC.
